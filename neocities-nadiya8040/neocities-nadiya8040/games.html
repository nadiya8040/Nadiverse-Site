Harminors

The game, based on and made as a successor of Millipede/Centipede, takes place in Planet Harmonica where players repel the Harminors in line and other Harmonsters.

Guest Appearance: Smitty (from SONAR Games, By Tetriser) , Ravia (by Vee Tee) , James (by Waver92) & Kinoko (from Cult of Galaxy)
